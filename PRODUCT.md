# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

World of Warcraft players, guildmates, and friends of Jeff Franzen who want to see who "WallHackJack" is — the character, the player, the raid history. Visited casually on desktop and phone, usually at night, from a Discord or chat link.

## Product Purpose

A cutting-edge personal fan/profile site for Jeff Franzen's WoW identity, WallHackJack. One page that reads like a legendary item tooltip come to life: character sheet, raid progression, achievements, and the player behind the name. Success = a guildmate opens the link and says "how was this made?"

## Brand Personality

Forged, heroic, arcane. The feel of a candle-lit armory at midnight — molten gold on black iron. Confident and playful (the name is a joke about a rogue who walks through walls), never corporate.

## Anti-references

- Official Blizzard/WoW UI clones or asset rips — this is a fan site, zero Blizzard assets.
- Generic esports templates: neon cyan/magenta gradients, aggressive angular panels, glitch text.
- SaaS landing-page grammar: hero-metric blocks, identical card grids, tracked uppercase eyebrows.

## Design Principles

1. **Legendary tooltip, not landing page.** The site borrows the *feeling* of loot rarity and character sheets, expressed through original design, not copied UI.
2. **The dark is the room.** Player at night, monitor glow — pure near-black surface, gold carries all warmth.
3. **Motion is arcane.** Particles, glows, and reveals feel like magic effects, purposeful and smooth, with full reduced-motion fallbacks.
4. **Made by hand, no build step.** Static HTML/CSS/JS that deploys anywhere and outlives its tooling.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA: body contrast ≥4.5:1 on the dark surface, reduced-motion alternative for every animation, semantic headings, keyboard-visible focus states.
